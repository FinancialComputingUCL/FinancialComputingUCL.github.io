The Conference

Unwinding Complexity 2023 Conference is an invitation-only event. It is organised on the occasion of Professor Tomaso Aste's 60th birthday, and it aims to gather together a heterogeneous community of co-authors and scientists who have worked on modelling Complex Systems, from materials to markets. This Conference represents a unique occasion to deepen the debate, discuss advances, and exchange ideas with leading experts in the field.

Topics Covered

Materials

  • Cellular matter, foams and emulsions
  • Granular matter
  • Glasses

Markets

  • Multivariate dynamics of financial systems and market states
  • Systemic risk in financial systems
  • Decentralized finance cryptocurrencies and tokenomics

Methodologies

  • Geometrical and topological approaches
  • Data representations in high dimensional/non-Euclidean spaces
  • Space-partitions, Voronoi tessellations and packings
  • Statistics of heavy-tailed distributions
  • Correlation, dependency, and causality structure
  • Sparse multivariate probabilistic modelling
  • Entropy-based modelling methods
  • Scaling laws and scaling exponents
